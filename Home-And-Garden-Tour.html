Home and Garden Tour

This annual event will feature an exciting and unique assortment of historic homes and gardens within the Leavenworth Area. Participants in the tour will be given the opportunity to visit each home and garden between noon and 5:00pm on the Sunday of Labor Day Weekend.
All of the proceeds from this event will go toward upgrading Cascade Medical’s current Endoscopy Suite.

Your participation will increase early detection and treatment of cancer in our community. ​
